My Promise我的承诺

Accessible. Supported. Heard. 为华生发声，照亮前行之路

1. Making Policy Accessible1. 让政策通俗易懂

I will push for clearer communication on Extenuating Circumstances (EC) and AI policies, focusing on international students who are often disadvantaged by complex language. 我将推动有关特殊情况申请 (EC) 和 AI 政策的更清晰沟通，重点关注因复杂的学术语言而处于劣势的国际学生。

2. Bridging the AI Gap2. 弥合 AI 使用差距

Using AI responsibly to manage time and preview materials. I want to be a student-University bridge, sharing what works for self-quizzing and previewing. 负责任地利用 AI 进行时间管理和材料预览。我想成为学生与大学之间的桥梁，分享有效的自测与预习方案。

3. Supporting Students Who Struggle Quietly3. 支持默默挣扎的学生

I took an interruption. For two years I was a student with special status — and I found my way through. Once you've stood in the heavy rain, you want to share your umbrella with the next person who needs it. 我曾办理过休学。在这所大学里，我有两年是以特殊身份就读的学生——最终我熬过来了。一旦你曾在暴雨中站立过，你就会想把伞分享给下一个需要它的人。
