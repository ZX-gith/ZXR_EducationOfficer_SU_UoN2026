Manifesto & Vision 🔑竞选宣言与愿景 🔑

Three Priorities.
One Direction.三大优先事项。
同一个方向。

Education should work for every student — not just those who already know how to navigate the system. 教育应该为每一位学生服务——而不仅仅是那些已经懂得如何在这个系统中生存的人。

01
Making Policy Accessible让政策通俗易懂

Policies like Extenuating Circumstances and AI Declaration exist to protect students — but only if they understand them. I will push for clearer communication, with particular attention to international and non-native English speaking students. 特殊情况申请 (EC) 和 AI 声明等政策的初衷是为了保护学生——前提是大家能看懂。我将推动更清晰的政策沟通，特别关注国际学生和非英语母语学生的痛点。

02
Bridging the AI Gap弥合 AI 使用差距

The University has pushed AI forward — but how to use it, where, and to what extent remains unclear. I've built my own approach and want to be a student-University bridge, so students can use AI confidently and responsibly. 大学在积极推进 AI，但具体如何使用、在哪里用、界限在哪里，依然模糊不清。我想成为学生与大学之间的桥梁，让大家能自信且负责任地使用 AI。

03
Supporting Students Who Struggle Quietly支持默默挣扎的学生

I took an interruption. For two years I was a student with special status at this University — and I found my way through. Once you've stood in the heavy rain, you want to share your umbrella with the next person who needs it. 我曾办理过休学。在这所大学里，我有两年是以特殊身份就读的学生——最终我熬过来了。一旦你曾在暴雨中站立过，你就会想把伞分享给下一个需要它的人。
