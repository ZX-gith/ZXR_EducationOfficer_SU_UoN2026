My Promise我的承诺

Accessible. Supported. Heard. 为华生发声，照亮前行之路

Education should work for every student — not just those who know how to navigate the system. 教育应该为每一位学生服务——而不仅仅是那些深谙系统规则的人。

Making Policy Accessible让政策通俗易懂

I will push for clearer communication on Extenuating Circumstances and AI policies, focusing on international students who are often disadvantaged by complex language. 我将推动有关特殊情况申请 (EC) 和 AI 政策的更清晰沟通，重点关注因复杂的学术语言而处于劣势的国际学生。

Bridging the AI Gap弥合 AI 使用差距

Using AI responsibly to manage time and preview materials. I want to be a student-University bridge, sharing what works. 负责任地利用 AI 进行时间管理和材料预览。我想成为学生与大学之间的桥梁，分享有效的学习方案。
